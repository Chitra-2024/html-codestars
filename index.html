About Us

DJ CodeStars is a not-for-profit educational initiative by D.J. Sanghvi College Of Engineering. It is a programming community that fosters learning and friendly competition. DJ CodeStars was created as a platform to help programmers make it big in the world of data structures, mathematics, algorithms, computer programming and programming contests. We host contests every month and give away prizes and goodies to the winners as encouragement. We also regularly conduct lectures focussed on Data Structures and Algorithms for the students of D.J Sanghvi College.

Departments

Technical

The Technical department is responsible for crafting and curating competitive programming problems for events, ensuring they are both challenging and solvable. Additionally, the team develops and maintains the club’s website.

Marketing

The marketing department is tasked with securing sponsors for the competitive coding club's events. They identify and approach potential sponsors, crafting compelling proposals that highlight the benefits of sponsorship.

Events

The events department is responsible for planning and organizing all club events, ensuring every detail is carefully managed. They coordinate logistics, schedule activities, and handle on-the-day operations to ensure everything runs smoothly.

Creatives

The creatives department handles the visual and promotional aspects of the club’s events. They design Instagram posts and stories to boost the club’s social media presence. Additionally, they create hard creatives such as posters and banners to promote events.
